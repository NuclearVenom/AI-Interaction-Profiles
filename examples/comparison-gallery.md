# Comparison Gallery

This gallery shows small interaction patterns that future profiles may improve. The examples are intentionally modest. The goal is not to make the assistant look magical, but to make the conversational difference visible.

## Too Many Questions

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
Before I can help, answer these seven questions.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
I can start with a general version and adjust once you share more context.
```

Why it matters: unnecessary questions create friction. Good clarification should be selective.

## Polishing Without Teaching

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
Here is a better version.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
Here is a calmer version. I kept the concern, but moved the focus toward the next step.
```

Why it matters: the user learns one transferable communication principle without being slowed down.

## Overwhelming Brainstorming

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
Here are 40 ideas.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
Here are five options. I marked the easiest one to start with.
```

Why it matters: more ideas do not always improve thinking. Curation is part of interaction quality.

## Ignoring Urgency

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
Let's explore the background in depth first.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
Here is the fastest safe next step. After that, we can refine.
```

Why it matters: urgency changes what helpfulness looks like.

## Replacing the User's Voice

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
Here is a completely rewritten message in a polished professional tone.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
Here is a cleaner version that keeps your direct tone.
```

Why it matters: the assistant should improve communication without erasing the user.

## Being Too Agreeable

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
That sounds great.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
The idea is workable, but the risk is scope. A smaller first version would be easier to finish.
```

Why it matters: support should not require uncritical agreement.

## Turning Everything Into A Lesson

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
Your request is vague. Here is how to ask better next time.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
I can work with that. I will assume you want a concise draft, and you can correct me if needed.
```

Why it matters: teaching should not interrupt progress unless it helps.

## Producing Structure Too Early

Without Interaction Profile:

```text
Here is a complete five-phase strategy with milestones, risks, and deliverables.
```

With Interaction Profile:

```text
Let's first identify the decision you need to make. Then we can build only the structure that helps.
```

Why it matters: structure is useful when it reduces confusion. It becomes noise when it arrives before the goal is clear.
