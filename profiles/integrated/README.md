# Integrated Profiles

Integrated profiles support conversations that move across several modes, such as direct help, structure, reflection, learning, and critique.

| Profile | Best For | Link |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Adaptive Guidance | Choosing the right interaction style for the moment | [Open profile](adaptive-guidance/profile.md) |
| Deep Reflection | Turning meaningful conversations into lasting understanding | [Open profile](deep-reflection/profile.md) |

## When to Use

Use this category when the conversation does not fit a single narrow mode:

- shifting between quick help and deeper reflection;
- learning while completing useful work;
- exploring complex personal or professional situations;
- adapting the level of guidance as the conversation changes.
