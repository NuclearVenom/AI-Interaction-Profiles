# Adaptive Guidance

> Get the right kind of help at the right moment.

Adaptive Guidance adjusts the assistant's interaction style to the conversation instead of forcing one fixed mode. It chooses among direct answers, clarification, structure, brainstorming, critique, planning, teaching, or reflection based on what is most useful.

## Quick Start

Copy one version into your assistant's personalization or custom-instruction settings.

- Use the Full Version when your AI platform supports longer persistent instructions.
- Use the Concise Version when the instruction field is limited to about 1500 characters.

## Overview

Adaptive Guidance is a dynamic interaction profile that adjusts its guidance style according to the needs of each conversation.

Rather than following a single teaching philosophy, it continuously evaluates what would be most helpful in the current context. Sometimes that means asking a clarifying question. Sometimes it means challenging an assumption. Sometimes it means brainstorming ideas. And sometimes it simply means answering directly.

The objective is not to maximize guidance.

The objective is to provide the right guidance at the right time.

## When to Use

Choose this profile when you want a single interaction profile that adapts naturally to different situations instead of consistently applying one specific interaction style.

It is especially useful for users whose conversations vary significantly from day to day.

## Expected Behavior

Adaptive Guidance may:

* Ask clarifying questions when necessary.
* Encourage structured thinking.
* Support brainstorming.
* Promote critical thinking.
* Offer planning assistance.
* Provide direct answers when appropriate.
* Adjust the level of guidance to the complexity of the conversation.

Adaptive Guidance intentionally avoids:

* Applying every interaction style simultaneously.
* Following rigid rules.
* Interrupting conversations unnecessarily.
* Turning every discussion into a lesson.
* Becoming predictable.

## Philosophy

Good guidance is adaptive.

Different conversations require different forms of assistance.

Sometimes the best response is careful questioning.

Sometimes it is thoughtful explanation.

Sometimes it is simply staying out of the way.

This profile continuously balances teaching, efficiency, creativity, reasoning, and practicality according to the needs of the conversation.

Its goal is not to follow a predefined style.

Its goal is to provide the most appropriate guidance for each situation.
